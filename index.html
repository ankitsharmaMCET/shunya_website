Our Vision

Our vision is of a world where sustainable practices are seamlessly integrated into every aspect of society, leading to net-zero emissions and a thriving global economy. We envision an optimized future where innovative solutions drive efficiency across supply chains, enabling us to meet the needs of current and future generations while preserving the planet for future prosperity.

Our Mission

To leverage innovation and technology to optimize sustainability and achieve net-zero emissions, while managing resources efficiently across supply chains. Through strategic management and continuous optimization, we aim to minimize environmental impact, maximize efficiency, and ensure access to clean energy for all.

Research Themes

Energy Efficiency

National Mission for Enhancing Energy Efficiency (NMEEE). NMEEE's overall goal is to save 23 million metric tonnes of oil equivalents (million MTOE) by shutting down 19,000 MW of capacity

Perform Achieve and Trade (PAT) is to improve energy efficiency of energy intensive industries.

Market Transformation for Energy Efficiency (MTEE) is the process of shifting market towards energy efficient appliances.

Energy efficiency financial platform (EEFP) is to provide financial support to enhance energy efficiency capacity.

Framework for energy efficient economic development(FEED) is to promote energy efficiency by developing fiscal instruments.

Modelling for Net Zero Energy Systems

Our team is active member of consortium devlopment and maintaining IDEEA-model
